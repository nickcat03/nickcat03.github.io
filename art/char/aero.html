Aero

Aero is a combination of a Zorua, Vulpix, Eevee, and probably many other things. He is a cute lil guy.

I am unsure if he is a dog or a fox. Maybe both? I guess he could be both.

Aero's design and name has changed several times, so a lot of the art I have of him vary.

Aero is one of my favorite characters. I would even dare say he is cuter than Brulee.

He is just a small.
