Brulee

Brulee was spawned one day out of nowhere as I was attempting to draw a Furret OC, which eventually offspringed into whatever this is.

He is an otter, or a ferret, or both.
You can figure it out.

Brulee also wears green goggles. I suck at drawing goggles so you can see them in the pics below by other artists.

I guess Brulee is a character that I would consider a "fursona". I enjoy using him as a way to represent myself on this website and other places on the internet.

He is a silly little guy, what more can I say.
